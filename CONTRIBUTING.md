# Contributing

Contributions are welcome — improvements to the guide, new templates, corrections, or better examples.

## How to contribute

1. Fork the repo
2. Make your changes on a branch
3. Open a Pull Request with a short description of what you changed and why

## What makes a good contribution

- **Improvements to `winning-with-claude.md`** — clearer explanations, better templates, additional patterns that work well in practice
- **Skill improvements** — better trigger descriptions, more robust setup flow in `SKILL.md`
- **Bug fixes** — broken links, outdated references, incorrect instructions

## What to avoid

- Bloating the guide with edge cases — keep it focused and practical
- Adding company- or project-specific content that doesn't generalise
- Large structural rewrites without prior discussion (open an issue first)

## Questions or ideas

Open an issue before starting significant work — easier to align upfront than after.
